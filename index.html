Mod Muse

Mod Muse is a visually engaging vintage fashion marketplace inspired by the bold and stylish aesthetic of the 1960s Mod era. The platform connects fashion lovers with curated vintage pieces, offering a seamless and inspiring shopping experience.

Designed in Figma, the app development involved defining key features, conducting competitive analysis, and refining the concept. I created user flows, wireframes, and prototypes, validating the design through A/B and user testing to ensure a seamless experience.
