About Me

My Story

I'm Kara, a UI/UX Designer based in Austin, Texas!

I have a background in retail, management, trend forecasting, digital strategy, and AI research. With a degree in fashion merchandising and over a decade of experience in styling—alongside experience in buying, consignment, and leadership—I’ve developed a deep understanding of consumer behavior, aesthetics, and data-driven decision-making—skills that naturally align with creating visually compelling, user-friendly interfaces with a strong UX foundation.

My background in styling, merchandising, and e-commerce has honed my ability to understand user needs and design solutions that balance form and function. Beyond design, I have a passion for clear, compelling copy and am expanding my skills in front-end development, allowing me to bridge the gap between design and implementation.

Additionally, I have experience researching AI and frequently integrate it into my workflow to enhance efficiency, optimize design processes, and create smarter, more adaptive user experiences. I thrive on crafting seamless, user-centered experiences that are both beautiful and functional.

Outside of work, I’m a devoted animal lover who’s usually with my dog, Joey, or indulging my passion for fashion—whether it’s sewing or getting creative styling a fun look!
