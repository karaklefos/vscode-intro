Life Anew Restorative Justice

The Dual-Platform Challenge: Optimizing Enterprise Workflows & Community Access

The Core Problem and Goal

  • Staff Lacked Centralized Support: Fragmented information access and the absence of a unified resource hub resulted in increased operational overhead and inconsistent service delivery.

  • Community Resource Crisis: Community members lacked a simple, digital pathway to access critical resources like shelter, food, and documentation.

  • The Opportunity: Create a single, interconnected mobile ecosystem that empowers staff and the community simultaneously.

Project Goal: Maximize organizational efficiency by drastically reducing employee training time and accelerating the speed at which essential services reach the community without navigating complex internal bureaucracy.
